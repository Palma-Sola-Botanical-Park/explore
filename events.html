See a 🔒 "Park closed" day above?

That could be your day.

We're a free park, open to the public almost every day of the year — so when you spot a "Park closed" note, that's a private event renting the grounds, and that rental quietly helps keep us free for everyone else. Here's the fun part: it could be your event. A wedding, a family reunion, your organization's fundraiser — ten acres of subtropical garden, exclusively yours for a day. Take a look at what we offer, and spread the word.
