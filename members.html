The park is free to everyone, every day — and it stays that way because of members like you. We take no government funding, so an annual membership directly keeps the gates open and the gardens growing.

Why Join

Your membership is the park's foundation

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit receiving no federal, state, or local funding, we rely on members to sustain and enhance these ten acres. Every level is tax-deductible under current IRS code — and every level now includes a Reciprocal Admissions Program card, opening the gates to 380+ botanical gardens nationwide.
